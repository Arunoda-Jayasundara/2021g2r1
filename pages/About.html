Team

We are a non profit organization that prides itself in being a pioneer in the English Language Teaching program in the whole country. We aim to equip and empower youngers from learning English as well as to clear out their fear to step out to use English in day today lifestyle. The organization is made up of qualified teachers, international volunteers and business professionals who are passionate about English language. Right from the start we have grown and will continue to do so as long as we continue to be a community of advocates for language learning. We offer free course contents which are designed to elementary kids to adults. Let's identify our team.
